Details

  • Assignment 1
    The aim of this assignment is to encode reasoning problems stated in English into classical logic. Prover9 should used to edit and test the representations. The required submission consists of Prover9 input files that are to be submitted to Gradescope.

    • The problems can be found here .

    • More information on using Prover9 can be found on my Prover9 exercises information web page.

    NOTE: The Gradescope submission link for Assignment 1 will not be available until a couple of days before the submission deadline.

  • Assignment 2
    Models and KRR Reasoning Methods.
    • Part A Individual work done online via Gradescope. Includes Sequent Caclculus, Models of a First-Order Logic Theory, Problem solving using a Prolog planner.
    • Part B Construction of Knowledge Base for a domain of your own choice. Can be done individually or in groups of 2 or 3. The full specification of this coursework piece is here .

  • Online Exam
    The test will cover all aspects of the module and will focus on the use of KRR representations to encode specific reasoning problems. This will be scheduled as a limited duration that must be taken within a longer time window. Full details will be given nearer the time. It is expected that the test should take 2-3 hours to complete, though you will probabaly be allowed a longer period in case of any issues arising.

    Some example past papers can be found here.
    Solutions are also provided. However it is strongly suggested that you attempt the questions before looking at the solutions.
