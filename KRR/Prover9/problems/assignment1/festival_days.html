Problem Set: assignment1

Festival Days

A logic puzzle by Brandon Bennett

Your problem is to use propositional logic to represent the following example of reasoning, and use the Prover9 theorem prover to prove that the reasoning is valid.

Use the following template file to create your Prover9 encoding of the problem: festival_days.p9

Marks: There are two marks for each sentence, making a total of 16 marks.

Outline: Why am I sad on a festival day? And what day is it anyway?

Problem Statement

A1 Today is a festival day but I'm not Happy.
A2 Festivals only take place on Moondays, Stardays and Sumdays.
A3 I'm always happy on Stardays
A4 On Sumdays I eat pancakes and on Moondays I eat jelly.
A5 On Moondays I sing and Dance
A6 When I'm ill I can neither sing nor dance.
A7 Eating pancakes or jelly makes me happy unless I'm ill.
Goal What day is it? *

Non-Logical Vocabulary

Propositions: FestivalDay, Happy, Moonday, Starday, Sumday, Sing, Dance, Ill, EatPancakes, EatJelly

Notes:

  • * The goal should be the answer to this quesiton.
